About Airalo
Airalo is the world's first eSIM store that helps people connect to the internet in 200+ countries and regions without the hassle of dealing with high roaming fees. Airalo connects its partners with millions of global travelers through its innovative and growing network of eSIMs.

About the Role
We are looking for a Data Engineering Manager to lead the foundational backend of our data organization. You will manage and mentor a team of data engineers, drive the architecture and delivery of our data infrastructure, and partner with analytics, product, and business teams to ensure high-quality data is available across the organization.

Responsibilities
- Lead, mentor, and grow a team of 5–8 data engineers across distributed time zones
- Own the architecture and roadmap for our data platform (pipelines, warehousing, orchestration)
- Partner with analytics, product, and business stakeholders to define and deliver data requirements
- Design and implement scalable ELT/ETL pipelines and data models
- Establish data quality frameworks, monitoring, and SLAs
- Drive the adoption of best practices in data engineering (testing, documentation, CI/CD)
- Collaborate with the Data Director on hiring, team development, and performance management
- Champion metadata-driven approaches to reduce pipeline development overhead
- Oversee migration and modernization of legacy data systems

Requirements
- 5+ years of data engineering experience, with 2+ years in a leadership or management role
- Strong proficiency in SQL and at least one data pipeline framework (dbt, Airflow, Spark, etc.)
- Experience designing and scaling cloud data warehouses (Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift, or Azure Synapse)
- Demonstrated ability to lead distributed teams and manage cross-functional stakeholders
- Experience with data migration projects and large-scale dataset handling (100M+ records)
- Familiarity with healthcare, fintech, or high-growth SaaS data environments is a plus
- Strong communication skills — ability to present technical concepts to executive audiences
- Experience integrating AI/LLM tooling into engineering workflows is a plus

Nice to Have
- Experience with Databricks, Delta Lake, or modern lakehouse architectures
- Background in metadata-driven ETL frameworks
- Familiarity with Kimball dimensional modeling methodology
