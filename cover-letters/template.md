# Cover Letter Template

> Use this as a starting point. The AI tool (`tools/generate_cover_letter.py`) will auto-generate tailored versions from your resume + a job description.

---

[Your Name]
[Your Email] | [Your Phone]
[City, State]
[Date]

[Hiring Manager Name, if known]
[Title]
[Company Name]
[Company Address]

---

Dear [Hiring Manager Name / Hiring Team],

**[Opening paragraph — Hook + why this role/company excites you]**

I am writing to express my strong interest in the [Job Title] position at [Company Name]. [1-2 sentences on why this company specifically — reference their mission, a product, recent news, or growth. Show you've done your homework.]

**[Body paragraph 1 — Your most relevant experience/achievement]**

In my role at [Previous/Current Company], I [specific achievement that maps to a key requirement in the JD]. This experience [connect to what they need].

**[Body paragraph 2 — Skills alignment + second achievement]**

[Second relevant experience or skill. Tie 1-2 more requirements from the JD to concrete examples from your background.]

**[Closing — Call to action]**

I am excited about the opportunity to bring my [key skills] to [Company Name] and contribute to [specific team goal or company mission]. I would welcome the chance to discuss how my background aligns with your needs. Thank you for your consideration.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]

---

*Tips:*
- *Keep it to one page / under 400 words*
- *Customize the opening and company-specific sentences for every application*
- *Run `python tools/generate_cover_letter.py --job "job_description.txt" --company "Acme" --role "Software Engineer"` for an AI-generated version*
